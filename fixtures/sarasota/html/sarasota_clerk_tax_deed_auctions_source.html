Tax Deed Auctions

In Sarasota County, Tax Deed auctions are conducted online Monday through Friday, excluding holidays, beginning at 9:00 A.M. EST/EDT

Tax Deed Auction Calendar

Buyers Beware: All properties are sold “as is.”

It is the bidder/buyer's responsibility to conduct due diligence research on each property and to fully understand the process, timelines, and risks involved when purchasing real property at auction.

It is the responsibility of the bidder/buyer to know what additional legal action may be required when purchasing real property for delinquent taxes. If you have questions about the process, you may want to consult an attorney

Auctions can be cancelled or postponed at any point.

Check the Auction Calendar for changes in auction status
